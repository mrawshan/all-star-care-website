Caregiver

If you’re compassionate and looking for a way to make a difference in the lives of local seniors, we encourage you to think about joining our team. At All Star Care, we hire loving and dependable caregivers who are committed to making a difference to seniors and their families. We provide non-­medical homecare to seniors and others who desire to stay at home. We offer per diem employment for hourly and live­-in positions as well as up to ­date and ongoing caregiver training and competitive pay. We offer the following benefits:

  • We work with your availability, whether it be part‐time, full‐time, days, nights or weekends.
  • We serve clients all over the Twin Cities area.
  • Professional, on‐going training and 24/7 administrative support.
  • Our wages are based on caregiver experience, the type of shift and the length of the shift
  • Bonus plus an employee referral program
