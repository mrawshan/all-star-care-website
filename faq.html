FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a minimum number of hours for a scheduled visit?

There are no minimum hours required per week.

How many caregivers do you have on staff?

As of January 2020, we have over 130 active caregivers on staff.

How long have you been in business?

All Star Care is a Colorado company serving the Twin Cities area since 2007.

Do you have a written policy and procedure manual and training program for employees?

Yes. Each caregiver receives a Personal Care Worker Handbook that is thoroughly reviewed upon hire. Each caregiver undergoes mandatory hands on skills training upon hire and annually completes a re-competency evaluation on client care, Alzheimer’s and Dementia care and a caregiver annual evaluation.

How are employees screened?

All employees go through pre-employment screening with a nationally recognized assessment tool, a thorough interview process, State and National criminal background checks, comprehensive personal and professional background checks, and competency verification and training.

What is the process for getting questions answered and issues resolved?

Simply call the office at (303) 346-1292. We have staff in the office five days a week and someone on call to answer urgent calls after hours and on weekends.

What are your billing procedures?

Invoices are sent every two weeks. Payment can be made by bank check, electronic check or credit card.?

How can I be sure of your reputation?

All Star Care is Home Care Pulse Certified and is licensed by CDPHE. References are available upon request?”

What if I need to change services or change my mind about getting help?

Changes can be made to the schedule or care plan at any time just by calling the office. If you should want to cancel services, all you need to do is call.

Are home care services covered by insurance or Medicare?

Our services can be funded by either private pay or through Long Term Care Insurance policies.

How quickly can services be started?

We prefer 2-3 days to complete our intake process. If the situation is urgent, we can often start on short notice.

What if a caregiver isn’t a good fit?

If the caregiver we send is not a good fit, we are happy to change to a different caregiver. We want all our clients to feel safe and comfortable with our caregivers.
